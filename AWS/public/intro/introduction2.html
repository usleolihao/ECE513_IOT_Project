Webhooks are a simple and flexible way to send data from your Particle devices to other apps and services around the Internet. Webhooks bridge the gap between the physical and the digital world, helping you get your data where you need it to be.



① In your particle firmware

Publish an event through the Particle Device Cloud that will be forwarded to all registered listeners, such as callbacks, subscribed streams of Server-Sent Events, and other devices listening

▶ Particle.publish() - Publish an event through the Particle Device Cloud that will be forwarded to all registered listeners, such as callbacks, subscribed streams of Server-Sent Events, and other devices listening

② How to create your webhook???

The hub for managing your webhooks is the Particle Console. Log into your console and click on the Integrations tab. If you have created any webhooks in the past, they will appear here. Click on "New Integration" -> "Webhook" to get started.

③ What value you will get?

▶ JSON data send to AWS
Particle.publish("smarthome",statusStr, PRIVATE)

▶ Variables:
- Simulated Clock
- Door Status
- Light Sensor Value
- Temperature
- Humidity
